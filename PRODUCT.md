# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, peers in technology leadership, potential collaborators and clients, maker/3D-printing enthusiasts, and curious visitors who arrive from LinkedIn, GitHub, the App Store, MakerWorld, or Cults3D. They want to quickly grasp who Alex Falcão is: a senior networks/infrastructure leader in banking who also builds products and prints in 3D.

## Product Purpose
A personal landing page (alexfalcao.pro.br) — a curated showcase, not an online CV. It communicates four things: who Alex is, what he builds, the products and research he develops, and how to reach him. Success = a visitor leaves understanding that Alex doesn't just administer technology, he turns knowledge into real products and solutions. Concrete evidence, not adjectives: the live AtivoSmart app (real in-app screenshots), the in-progress Garrafa Certa, the master's research (HealthRank / UNIFESP), and a decade of real 3D prints.

## Brand Personality
Precise, nocturnal, initiatory. Senior authority from three decades in critical banking infrastructure, balanced with the curiosity of a maker (apps, 3D printing) and a discreet esoteric thread — the strophalos of Hecate, wheel of crossroads and thresholds. Voice: calm, confident, unhurried. The site headline is "Construo caminhos." (I build paths), and the closing motto is "Abrir caminhos é remover obstáculos" (opening paths is removing obstacles) — the literal through-line, since networks route paths and the strophalos opens crossroads.

## Anti-references
Navy-and-gold fintech template. Generic dark SaaS landing page. Editorial-magazine display-serif-plus-italic-drop-caps affectation. Costume-y occult clip art. Online-CV bullet dumps. Overused AI-default fonts (Inter, Fraunces, Space Grotesk, IBM Plex).

## Design Principles
- **Paths, not decoration.** Every esoteric or network motif earns its place by carrying the "opening paths" idea.
- **Senior restraint over flash.** The gravitas comes from precision and pacing, not effects.
- **Show the maker.** Products, research, and 3D work are concrete evidence, not adjectives.
- **Discreet mysticism.** The strophalos is a faint signature (watermark, slow rotation), never a theme park.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast on all text. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` support (strophalos rotation, counters, reveals all degrade to static/instant). Content visible without JS; motion only enhances.
